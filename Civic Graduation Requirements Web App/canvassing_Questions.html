Canvassing

Canvassing is when you engage in direct contact with people who you seek to influence.
Generally this is done in the form of canvassing on the street: stopping people and talking to them,
or door to door canvassing when you pick a location or building and talk to residents.
If you are successful at this then you can easily interact with citizens on the street or in their homes to
discuss with them a certain platform.
