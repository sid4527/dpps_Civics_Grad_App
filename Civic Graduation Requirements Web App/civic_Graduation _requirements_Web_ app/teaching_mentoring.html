Teaching & Mentoring

Teaching involves working one on one or with a group of people to teach them a skill (such as reading comprehension,
resume building, etc). Mentoring involves working one on one or with a group of people to provide them guidance,
leadership skills or serve as a role model.
If you are successful with this then the group you worked with will have learned a skill set.
