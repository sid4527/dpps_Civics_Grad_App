Cause-Related Fundraising

Cause related fundraising involves raising funds or in-kind donations for a cause or an
organization other than yourself or a family member. Examples include raising money for
cancer, a school dance, gun violence awareness, etc. If you are successful at this then you
feel comfortable designing a plan to raise
money, can execute that plan and complete any compliance documents related to the fundraising.
